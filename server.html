Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server?
Just hit "Play Now" or throw the IP `scarletsmp.mc-join.xyz` into your Java Edition server list. That's literally it.
What version does the server run?
We're on 1.21.11, Java only. Bedrock's not supported so don't ask lol.
How does Lifesteal work?
Everyone starts with 10 hearts. Kill someone, take a heart. Simple. Lose all your hearts though and you're done, eliminated for the whole season.
Do I lose hearts if I die to a mob?
Nah, mobs can't touch your heart count. Only players can do that. So a creeper blowing up in your face just costs you some XP, not your life.
How do I get a rank?
Head to the Store page and hit "Ask on Discord". Ranks go through the founder directly, not some automated shop.
Are there any banned mods?
Yeah and the list is pretty long so pay attention. Banned: hacked clients, movement mods, ItemScroller, MouseTweaks, radar/minimap/world map mods, ESP of any kind, freecam, replay mod, accurate block placement mods, macros, scripts, auto clickers, crystal optimizers (Marlow and Hero are fine though), X-ray mods or resource packs, bridging mods including bedrock bridging, Inventory Profile Next, and ProfileMacros. General rule: if it gives you an advantage it's probably not allowed. Check the Server Rules page if you're unsure.
Can I play with my friends?
Obviously! Get your whole friend group in, make an alliance, and go bully everyone else on the leaderboard together.
How do I get more hearts?
Kill people and take theirs, that's the whole point of the gamemode. If PvP isn't really your thing, Heart Boosters in the store work too. They're permanent and they stack so they're actually worth it.
What happens if I break a rule?
Depends how bad it is. Could be a warning, could be a mute, could be a permanent ban. Staff have the final say so just don't do it.
Is there a Discord server?
Yeah of course, join it. Widget's right up top or there's a link in the footer. That's where everything happens, announcements, updates, reports, all of it.
How do I contact a staff member?
Discord is the move, always. If the founder's online in-game you can DM them there too but Discord is way faster.
What is the server IP?
