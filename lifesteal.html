❤️ So, how does Lifesteal actually work?

The basics

  • Everyone joins with 10 hearts. That's your life total.
  • Kill another player? You steal one of their hearts. It becomes yours permanently.
  • You die? You lose a heart to whoever killed you. Every death hurts.
  • The last player with at least one heart left wins the season. That's the whole point.
